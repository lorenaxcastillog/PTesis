 This review is not about the toy, but about the service you can expect to receive from Amazon.com. If you are buying this as a gift, only do so if you have months to wait, even if the item is listed as available. Amazon.com will allow you to pay your money under the guise of sending your items, and then days later, inform you of a delay in shipping, and then a week later inform you the item has just shipped and can take up to an additional 10 days for delivery. Despite the site's claims that the item would ship in 24hrs and be delivered in 3-5 days. Mind you, they're sending it via UPS or American Home.... Honestly, there are other retailers out there who are more reliable than Amazon.com and I strongly recommend using them - unless you're okay disappointing the little girl in your life.
