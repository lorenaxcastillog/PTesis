 At a year and a half, our daughter went through a phase where she wanted Mommy's keys ALL THE TIME. Enter this key ring, so she could have her own keys. Good points: - They have metal-looking keys that klink, instead of plastic obviously-fake keys. - They're easy to handle - The sounds and lights are fun during car trips. Only downside: The sounds are very REALISTIC. Grandma's gone to get the door a few times when the door button pushed. Dad freaked out when driving in the car, she pushed the honking horns/traffic noise. A tip: She didn't want to play with them until I pretended to use them on the car and the house.
