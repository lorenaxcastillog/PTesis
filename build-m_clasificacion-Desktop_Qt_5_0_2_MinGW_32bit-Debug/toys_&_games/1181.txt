 This climber is awesome, the kids love it, not only mine but all of the neighborhood ones too. My 1 year old was climbing through the tunnel before we even put it on the tower. The only glitch was putting together. 4 grown men were trying to get these walls to get together. They just kept coming apart. We took it back, rather than spend 3 hours and use a screwdriver, like some of the previous reviewers. We also spoke to Little Tikes. They suggested taking it back, as the notches and holes just would not stay together. If it is difficult, take it back, the new one went together with no trouble!
