 I bought the Lego set of Dora and Boots at Play Park for my child care home. The 4 toddlers I care for are very happy with it. Unfortunately, I can't seem to find extra little Dora and/or Boots figures. Seems as though those specific pieces are what really draws their attention; the set comes with just one of each main character. So far, the pieces have held up very well - I often find 2 or even 3 little ones trying to either keep or take one of these pieces. So far, they've withstood the tug of war. I bought the set at a Christmas sale and paid just half price. However, now that I want to get another set, I've found, much to my dismay, that the retail price is $24.95. Too much for a few popular pieces of plastic
