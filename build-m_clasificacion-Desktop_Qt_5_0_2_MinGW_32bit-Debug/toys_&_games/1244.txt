 So uncomplicated that our six-year old can play, but so well-designed that it remains fun for our teenagers as well as for us parents. Acquiring resources is just hard enough that you must cooperate with the other players - there's no way just to "go around GO" and accumulate property - you've got to maintain a trading posture throughout the game. Your choices of how to develop are close, and since the board representing the geography of the island is set up differently each time, players need to be flexible in approaching each session. Several expansion kits are available, "Cities of Catan" is one we purchased and enjoyed
