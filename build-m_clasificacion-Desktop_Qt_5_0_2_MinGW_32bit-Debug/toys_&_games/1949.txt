 If your child enjoys acting out the Thomas & Friends episodes, 'Arry and Bert are known as the "Grim Messengers" because they love scrapping down old steam engines at the smelting yard. In fact, their goal in life is to scrap down all the steam engines because they think that diesels can do everything better. To expand the fun of these diesels, you can get the new smelting yard, which is the "hottest" destination on Sodor. You can load the unique glowing red cargo inside 'Arry and Bert's railway cars. If your child loves scrapping down old steam engines, then 'Arry, Bert and the smelting yard are MUST HAVES
