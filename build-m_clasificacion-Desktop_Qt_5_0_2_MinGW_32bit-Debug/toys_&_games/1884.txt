 My grandchildren loved this game from the first time we brought it out of the "game closet". Ages 6 and 9 and Grandpa and Grandma had a wonderful time ! It's a little difficult at first, as all really good games are, but well worth the time spent. The children were very quick to find the various "things". It is a good mental focus style of game and one that makes a child look and really "see". We recommended this to another family whose children couldn't seem to settle down and focus.
