 This just might be one of the coolest games out there. Sure, the box itself might make this game look SOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOO boring, but once you actually play the game it's like FOOSH and you're in a different world. I mean this is AWESOME. It's also really durable as I've had it for about a year now and only the box has a slight tweak and that's all. Although this "for ages 8 and up" which is SO lame, I think it should be for kids of all ages. Why? Because it is so easy to learn. It only took me like 2 minutes to learn it well and I am 9 years old. Anyway, I STRONGLY suggest you buy this game for your kids or anybody who really likes strategy stuff. 'Later...
