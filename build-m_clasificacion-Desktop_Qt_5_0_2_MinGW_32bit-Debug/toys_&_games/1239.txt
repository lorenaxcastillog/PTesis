 This is great. It really gives some class to yahtzee- no red plastic cup making alot of noise while someone rattles the dice. Otherwise, the game is the same. I liked this version. It also looks attractive on a bookshelf rather than a loud red box. One suggestion as an alternative- if you want the game to travel and you cannot find anyone who loves the game as much as you do, check out the electronic version.
