 If you've seen CARS, the animated movie, you know all about the tow truck called Mater, voiced by Larry The Cable Guy. This soft plush toy has his voice saying one of 75 different phrases when you squeeze his tow hook or front bumper. Some of the phrases are two-parters where Mater will ask a question and wait a few seconds for an answer, then add another quote. It's not really interactive, but will seem so to a child. Great toy for a kid of any age - I got it as a gag gift for my son, who is 31 years old. It cracked him up
