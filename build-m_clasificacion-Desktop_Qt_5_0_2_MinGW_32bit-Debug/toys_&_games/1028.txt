 If your child enjoys acting out the Thomas & Friends series, Rusty is a hard-at-work, heavy-duty diesel. He is a small orange diesel who pulls a dumper that carries removable sand cargo that can work with Cranky or other Thomas cranes. The cement mixer is unique. It spins as it is pulled, almost as if it is mixing cement. To "build" the fun, you can make buildings and bridges with Legos, Lincoln logs, or even the new Sodor blocks sets, and Rusty will bring the construction supplies you need. To "build" the fun even more, you can expand the Thomas wooden rail and road system with the new Cement Works, as well as Jack and Alfie from the construction crew. Then your construction series will be complete. Rusty is a perfect addition to the Thomas wooden rail and road system. After all, he's the one who helps build them
