 We used these bottles for three weeks, and we are very skeptical that they actually work as well as they are supposed to. There are some additional drawbacks as well. 1) These leak in travel. Twice we've gone to the park with 7oz, and arrived with 4 and a mess in the bag. 2) We see no difference in reducing gas. And while these are designed to take gas away from the collar on, what about all the air in the nipple where the air first enters? Is this a gimmic? 3) The flow is too fast. We went from bottles with smaller holes and less flow to these, which are far too fast for an infant
