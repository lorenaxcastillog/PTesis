 I picked this car seat because it got high marks on the Consumer Report tests and it was very reasonably priced. I was confident in my choice after reading customer reviews here and on Target.com. I have a 2-door Civic and placed the seat forward-facing in the middle of the back seat. Adults can sit next to it but it is a little cramped. Because of the seat styling in the Civic I had to put rolled up towels under the sides of the base to stabilize it but I had that problem with the infant seat.
