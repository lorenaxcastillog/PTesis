 The Snuggle Nest gave my husband and I peace of mind while co-sleeping with our son. I thought about purchasing the Arm's Reach Co-sleeper but my son wanted to be REAL close to us through the night. Our son is ten weeks old and it still works. It is cumbersome when trying to nurse because you have to sit up and pull the baby out and after a Cesarean that proved to be a little difficult. I'm hoping the transition to his crib will be easy with the Snuggle Nest by putting the Snuggle Nest into his crib. The little light is helpful when trying to get my son to latch on during nursing or chaning diapers in the middle of the night (the bedside light wakes my husband.
